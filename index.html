WELCOME TO EESY

A DIGITAL PHOTOBOOTH EXPERIENCE


1. TAKE PHOTOS

Allow camera access. Click the camera icon to take 4 photos.


2. DECORATE WITH
FRAMES AND STICKERS

Choose a frame from the panel on the right. Click on a sticker to make it appear near your photos. Drag them around and decorate.


3. CAPTION YOUR CREATION

Add a caption to finish your masterpiece. Take a screenshot to save it.
